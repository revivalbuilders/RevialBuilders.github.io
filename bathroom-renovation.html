Curbless Showers

Our specialty is creating beautiful, accessible curbless showers that combine modern aesthetics with practical functionality. Curbless showers eliminate the traditional step-over barrier, creating a seamless transition that's both elegant and accessible.

Perfect for aging-in-place designs, modern aesthetics, or simply maximizing your bathroom's visual appeal.

  • Zero-threshold entry for accessibility
  • Custom tile work & waterproofing
  • Built-in niches with LED lighting
  • Linear drains & proper sloping
  • Glass enclosures & built-in benches
